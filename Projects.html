RIPPLE

2D-Animation Film |
Editing

A small change, like an unexpected shift, sets off a chain reaction, turning a calm day into chaos. What seems minor at first can ripple outward, spiraling into bigger, unpredictable consequences, reflecting how tiny disruptions can completely alter our plans.

My everyday life, animated in chaos.

The 2D animation brings Ira’s chaotic day to life, using movement and color to express her e motions and frustrations. It immerses the audience in the ups and downs of her day, highlighting the chaos she experiences.

PLAY

Design | Packaging Design

PLAY combines classic gaming nostalgia with modern excitement. Targeting teens, its pocket consoles feature iconic games in a vibrant, arcade-inspired design. The bold packaging and "I Would Rather Play" title offer an immersive, fun-filled escape from boredom.

Redefining classic gaming for a new generation

The PLAY brand was born through a custom pixel-style font, inspired by retro gaming. This geometric design reflects the brand’s nostalgic yet modern vibe, setting the tone for a vibrant, immersive gaming experience.

OK COMPUTER

Redesigning Album cover |
Poster Design

Radiohead’s "OK Compute" looks at isolation, modern life, and how technology affects people. With emotional lyrics and unique sounds, it questions consumerism and disconnection, making it a powerful and influential album loved by many.

Connecting Romanticism to today’s disconnection.

The "OK Computer" artwork captures alienation and technological disillusionment. The pixelating *Monk by the Sea* and dark cityscape evoke isolation, detachment, and despair, highlighting how technology erodes our connection to nature and self.
